R Natural Numbers

A natural numbers they start from 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 ..... upto infiniite.

Whole Numbers

The whole numbers starts from 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 ..... upto infiniite.

Integers

Both numbers positive and negative numbers are Integers.
For example, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5...etc are positive numbers and -1, -2, -3, ....etc are negative numbers.

Prime Numbers

A number is divisible by 1 and itself that is Prime numbers.

Composite Numbers

Except prime numbers all are composite numbers.
A composite numbers has more than 2 factors.
For example, 2, 4, 6, 8 and so on.
